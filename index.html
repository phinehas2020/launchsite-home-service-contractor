Our Process

How we operate.

  1. 01

    Inspect before promising

    We inspect first, then speak in specifics: likely causes, options, budget ranges, and timeline risks.

  2. 02

    Write scope in plain language

    We spell out what is included, what is excluded, and what could change if hidden conditions appear.

  3. 03

    Protect the home while working

    Drop cloths, zip walls, daily cleanup, and respectful crew behavior are non-negotiable standards.

  4. 04

    Verify before signoff

    We test, review with you, and hand over final notes so you know exactly what was done and why.
